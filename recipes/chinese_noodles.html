Chinese Noodles

Best Chinese Noodles Recipe Ever

Slippery Lo Mein Noodles tossed in a Lo Mein Sauce with tons of veggies and protein of your choice. Takeout gold standard comes down to the right sauce and the right noodles – then you’re just 6 minutes away from noodle heaven!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 tbsp vegetable or peanut oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 medium carrots
  • 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • 500g noodles

Steps

  1. Sauce: Mix cornflour and dark soy until lump free, then add remaining Sauce ingredients.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large heavy based skillet over high heat until smoking.
  3. Add onion and garlic, stir 30 seconds.
  4. Add carrot and capsicum/bell peppers, cook 2 minutes or until chicken is cooked.
  5. Add noodles, Sauce and water. Use 2 wooden spoons and toss for 30 seconds.
  6. Add green onions, toss for another 1 minute until all the noodles are slick with sauce.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with extra green onions if using.
